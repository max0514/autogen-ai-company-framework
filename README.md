# 🚀 100-Minute AI Startup Builder | Google AI Workshop

Welcome to the official repository for the **AI Startup Builder** workshop! 

In 2026, launching a startup doesn't require a massive engineering and marketing team on day one. With the right "AI Entrepreneurship Framework," you can build an entire company from scratch in just 100 minutes. 

This repository contains the Microsoft AutoGen scripts and Google Gemini prompts used to simulate a fully autonomous AI boardroom, taking an idea from market research to a deployed product and final marketing assets.


## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Brain:** Google Gemini (1.5 Flash / Pro)
* **Multi-Agent Framework:** Microsoft AutoGen
* **Frontend/Backend Deployment:** Google AI Studio (Vibe Coding) & Firebase (Project IDX)
* **Visuals & Media:** Nano Banana (Images), Veo (Video), Lyria 3 (Music)
